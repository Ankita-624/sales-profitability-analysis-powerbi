# Sales & Profitability Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability trends, regional business performance, and product-level insights. The dashboard enables business users to monitor KPIs, identify operational inefficiencies, and support data-driven strategic decisions.

The project focuses on analytical storytelling through interactive visualizations, decomposition trees, and profitability analysis.

---

## Objectives
- Monitor sales and profitability KPIs
- Identify loss-making products and categories
- Analyze regional business performance
- Improve reporting clarity and business visibility
- Support operational and strategic decision-making

---

## Key Insights
- Certain product categories contributed to consistent losses
- Regional sales performance varied significantly across markets
- High sales volume did not always translate into profitability
- Root cause analysis highlighted operational inefficiencies affecting margins
